Privacy Policy — Tab Sentence

Last updated: April 2025

Tab Sentence is developed and maintained by Jeremiah Roper, trading as Jeremiah Bullfrog Music (ABN provided on request), based in Victoria, Australia.

Information We Collect

Tab Sentence does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information. The app operates entirely offline and stores all data locally on your device only.

Data Stored on Your Device

The app stores the following data locally on your device using your device's built-in storage:

  • Your current tab sentence input
  • Your selected tuning
  • Your saved tabs list
  • Your display preferences (dark mode, line wrap settings)

This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to the developer or any third party.

Third-Party Services

Tab Sentence does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

The app contains a link to buymeacoffee.com/Bullfrog. Tapping this link opens an external website in your browser. That website has its own privacy policy which is independent of this app.

Children's Privacy

Tab Sentence does not collect any data from any users, including children. The app is suitable for all ages.

Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy changes in the future, the updated version will be posted at this URL and the "Last updated" date will be revised.
